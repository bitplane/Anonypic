Anonypic for Android

What is it?

Anonypic is a way to share images using bayimg, the anonymous, uncensored image sharing service by the people behind The Pirate Bay.

Why do I need it?

It lets you upload images from your phone while retaining your privacy, your phone stores information like your location and phone information in the EXIF metadata held inside photos. If you want to remain anonymous you need to remove all this data.

Your site has ads, doesn't this defeat the purpose?

The view image page may have an advert on it, but the image is sent to the page via HTTP POST, so the ad provider can't actually see which image you uploaded.

Aren't you stealing bayimg's ads then?

The intermediate page exists so that their ad providers can't see which image you uploaded. When you share the link it does take you to bayimg's website containing their ads. My ads are just for the uploader.
